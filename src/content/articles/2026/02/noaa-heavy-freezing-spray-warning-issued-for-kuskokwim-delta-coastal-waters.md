### Alert Details

A Heavy Freezing Spray Warning has been issued by the National Weather Service in Anchorage, AK. The alert is classified as a severe threat with a high level of certainty. Additionally, a Gale Warning remains in effect for the region through Thursday.

### Affected Areas

The warning specifically covers the Kuskokwim Delta from 15 to 80 NM (Marine Forecast Zone PKZ764).

### What You Should Do

Mariners and residents in the affected coastal areas are advised to avoid the warning zone. Heavy freezing spray can cause significant ice accumulation on vessels, creating hazardous operating conditions.

### Expected Conditions

Weather conditions in the ice-free waters are expected to include:

*   **Thursday:** West winds at 35 knots with seas reaching 11 feet. Heavy freezing spray is expected during this period.
*   **Wednesday/Wednesday Night:** South to southwest winds at 35 knots with seas between 13 and 16 feet. Snow showers are expected Wednesday night.
*   **Thursday Night:** West winds decreasing to 30 knots with seas at 10 feet.

### Timeline

The Heavy Freezing Spray Warning is effective starting at 5:00 AM AKST on Thursday, February 12, 2026. The warning is scheduled to expire at 5:00 PM AKST on Thursday, February 12, 2026.
