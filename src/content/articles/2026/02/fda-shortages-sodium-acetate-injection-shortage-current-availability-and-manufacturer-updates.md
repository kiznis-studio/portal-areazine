# Sodium Acetate Injection Shortage Update

### What's in Shortage
**Generic Name:** Sodium Acetate Injection  
**Brand Name:** SODIUM ACETATE  
**Dosage Form:** Injection  
**Therapeutic Category:** Total Parenteral Nutrition  
**Current Status:** Current Shortage  
**Initial Posting Date:** March 23, 2016  
**Last Updated:** February 5, 2026

### Which Manufacturers Are Affected
The following manufacturer is listed in the FDA database regarding this shortage:

*   **Fresenius Kabi USA, LLC**
    *   **Status:** Available
    *   **Presentation:** Sodium Acetate, Injection, 4 meq/100 mL (NDC 63323-032-00)
    *   **Notes:** The manufacturer advises checking wholesalers for current inventory.
    *   **Contact Information:** 888-386-1300

### Why There's a Shortage
No specific reason for the shortage was provided in the source data.

### What Patients Should Do
If you or a loved one relies on Sodium Acetate Injection as part of Total Parenteral Nutrition, please consider the following steps:

*   **Consult Your Healthcare Provider:** Discuss your treatment plan with your doctor. Do not make any changes to your medical regimen without professional guidance.
*   **Talk to Your Pharmacist:** Pharmacists can provide real-time updates on local stock and may be able to coordinate with wholesalers to secure supply.
*   **Contact the Manufacturer:** For specific questions regarding supply, you may reach Fresenius Kabi USA at 888-386-1300.
