### What Happened
Brinkley RV has initiated a recall for certain 2026 Model Z trailers due to a defect in the electrical system. According to the manufacturer, the shore power inlet lugs may have been improperly tightened during production.

### Which Products Are Affected
The recall affects a total of 48 units. The specific vehicles involved are:
* **Make/Model**: 2026 Brinkley RV Model Z
* **NHTSA Campaign Number**: 26V043000
* **Manufacturer Recall Number**: REC-2601

### What You Should Do
Owners of the affected trailers are advised to contact an authorized Brinkley RV dealer. Dealers will inspect the shore power inlet lugs and tighten them to the correct specifications, free of charge. Owner notification letters were mailed on February 2, 2026. For additional information or questions, consumers can reach Brinkley customer service at 1-574-501-4280.

### Why This Matters
Improperly secured electrical lugs can cause wires to loosen and overheat during use. This condition significantly increases the risk of a fire, posing a danger to occupants and the vehicle.
