### What Happened
Grand Design RV, LLC has issued a recall for certain 2026 recreational vehicles due to a defect in the plumbing system's freeze prevention equipment. The affected vehicles are equipped with a ThermaHeat Tank Pad that may experience high resistance and fail. According to the manufacturer, a failure in this heating pad can lead to an increased risk of fire.

### Which Products Are Affected
The recall impacts approximately 300 units in total. The following models are included in this recall:
*   **2026 Grand Design Lineage**
*   **2026 Grand Design Solitude**

These vehicles are specifically those equipped with the ThermaHeat Tank Pad component.

### What You Should Do
Owners of the affected vehicles are advised to park their units and park them outside until the issue is addressed. Grand Design RV is currently developing a remedy for this defect. 

Interim owner notification letters are expected to be mailed on March 11, 2026. Consumers with questions may contact Grand Design customer service at **1-574-825-9679**. When calling, refer to Grand Design's internal recall numbers: **910055** or **M910056**.

### Why This Matters
The failure of the water tank heating pad poses a significant safety hazard as it can cause a fire, potentially resulting in property damage or serious injury to occupants.
