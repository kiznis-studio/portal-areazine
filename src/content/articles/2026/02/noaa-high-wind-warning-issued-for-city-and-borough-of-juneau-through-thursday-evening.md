### Alert Details
The National Weather Service in Juneau has issued a High Wind Warning for the region. The alert was issued at 1:01 PM AKST and remains in effect until 6:00 PM AKST this evening, February 12, 2026.

### Affected Areas
This weather alert specifically impacts the City and Borough of Juneau.

### What You Should Do
Residents and mariners are urged to take the following precautions:
* Secure vessels and any loose outdoor objects that could be blown around or damaged by the wind.
* Prepare for potential power outages.
* Exercise caution if travel is necessary, as conditions will be difficult for land, sea, and air transit.
* Report any wind-related damage to the National Weather Service by visiting weather.gov/Juneau/StormReports.

### Expected Conditions
* **Wind Speed**: Southeast winds sustained between 25 to 35 mph.
* **Wind Gusts**: Peak gusts are expected to reach up to 60 mph.
* **Hazards**: High winds are expected to blow around unsecured objects, damage property, and cause power outages.

### Timeline
The High Wind Warning is effective immediately and is scheduled to expire at 6:00 PM AKST this evening, February 12, 2026.
