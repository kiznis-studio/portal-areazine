## What's in Shortage
Quinapril Hydrochloride (Brand Name: QUINAPRIL) in tablet form is currently listed on the FDA drug shortage database. This medication is categorized under cardiovascular treatments. The shortage was first posted on January 19, 2023, and the status was last updated on February 2, 2026.
