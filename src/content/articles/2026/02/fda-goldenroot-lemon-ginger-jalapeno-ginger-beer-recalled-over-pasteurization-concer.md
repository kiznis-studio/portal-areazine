### What Happened
Weaver's Brew LLC has initiated a voluntary recall of its Goldenroot Lemon Ginger Jalapeno Ginger Beer. The recall was issued because the ginger juice used in the beverage may not have undergone the required pasteurization process, which is a critical safety step in juice-based food production.

### Which Products Are Affected
The recall affects approximately 421 cases of the following product distributed in **Texas** and **California**:

*   **Product Name**: Goldenroot Lemon Ginger Jalapeno Ginger Beer
*   **Size**: 12 oz. (355 ml)
*   **UPC**: 8 50052-32820 7
*   **Code Information**: All "Use By" dates up to and including 6/14/26
*   **Manufacturer**: Goldenroot (Weaver's Brew LLC), 3841 Market St #113, Denton, TX 76209

### What You Should Do
Consumers who have purchased the affected Goldenroot Ginger Beer are advised not to consume the product. The manufacturer initiated the recall notification via email. For questions regarding the recall or to inquire about returns, consumers can contact Weaver's Brew LLC at their Denton, Texas address: 3841 Market St Ste 113, Denton, TX 76209.

### Why This Matters
Pasteurization is a heat-treatment process designed to kill harmful bacteria and pathogens in food and beverages. Consuming juice products that have not been properly pasteurized can pose a health risk to consumers.
