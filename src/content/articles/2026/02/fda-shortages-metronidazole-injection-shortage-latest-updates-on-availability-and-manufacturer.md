## What's in Shortage
Metronidazole Injection, a medication in the anti-infective therapeutic category, is currently listed as being in shortage. This shortage affects the injection dosage form, specifically the 500 mg/100 mL presentation. Brand names associated with this medication include METRONIDAZOLE and Flagyl I.v. Rtu.

## Which Manufacturers Are Affected
According to the FDA database, the following manufacturer information is available:

*   **Baxter Healthcare**: Currently listed as "Available."
    *   **Presentation**: Flagyl I.v. Rtu In Plastic Container, Injection, 500 mg/100 mL (NDC 0338-1055-48)
    *   **Contact Information**: 888-229-0001

## Why There's a Shortage
The source data does not provide a specific reason for the current shortage of Metronidazole Injection.
