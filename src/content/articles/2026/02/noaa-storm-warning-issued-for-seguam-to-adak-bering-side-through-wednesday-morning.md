### Alert Details

The National Weather Service in Anchorage, AK, has issued a Storm Warning for the Seguam to Adak Bering Side. This alert is effective as of February 10, 2026, at 2:25 PM AKST and remains in effect until February 11, 2026, at 5:00 AM AKST.

### Affected Areas

The warning specifically covers the Seguam to Adak Bering Side from 15 to 85 nautical miles (NM) out. This includes the marine zone PKZ781.

### What You Should Do

Mariners and residents in the affected area are advised to avoid the region. The NWS response instruction for this event is to avoid the hazard area due to severe conditions.

### Expected Conditions

According to the Coastal Waters Forecast, the following conditions are expected:

*   **Tonight**: West winds at 50 knots, diminishing to 40 knots after midnight. Seas are expected to reach 24 feet. Widespread rain and snow showers are anticipated.
*   **Wednesday**: West winds at 40 knots with seas of 22 feet. Widespread snow showers will continue.
*   **Wednesday Night**: Northwest winds at 40 knots with seas remaining at 22 feet.
*   **Extended Outlook**: Conditions are expected to gradually subside through Thursday and Friday, with winds dropping to 30-35 knots and seas decreasing to 12-17 feet.
