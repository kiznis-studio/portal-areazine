### Alert Details

*   **Alert Type**: High Wind Warning
*   **Issuing Agency**: National Weather Service (NWS) Juneau AK
*   **Effective Period**: From 11:43 AM AKST Wednesday, February 11, until 6:00 PM AKST Thursday, February 12.

### Affected Areas

This warning specifically impacts the following regions in Alaska:
*   Petersburg Borough
*   City and Borough of Wrangell

### What You Should Do

Residents and operators in the warning area are urged to take the following precautions:
*   Secure vessels and any loose outdoor objects that could be blown around or damaged by high winds.
*   Prepare for potential power outages and property damage.
*   Exercise caution as travel by land, sea, or air will be difficult during this window.
*   Report any wind-related damage to the National Weather Service by visiting weather.gov/Juneau/StormReports.

### Expected Conditions

*   **Wind Speed**: Sustained south winds of 25 to 35 mph.
*   **Wind Gusts**: Peak gusts are expected to reach up to 60 mph.
*   **Impacts**: High winds are likely to blow around unsecured objects, damage property, and cause power outages. Travel conditions are expected to be hazardous across all modes of transportation.

### Timeline

*   **Onset**: The period of highest concern begins at 3:00 AM AKST on Thursday, February 12.
*   **Duration**: The warning remains in effect through 6:00 PM AKST Thursday evening.
