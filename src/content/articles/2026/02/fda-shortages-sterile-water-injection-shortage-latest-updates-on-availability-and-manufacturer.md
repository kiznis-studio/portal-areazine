## What's in Shortage
Sterile Water Injection (brand name: STERILE WATER) is currently listed in a shortage status. This product is an injection used within the "Other" therapeutic category. The shortage was initially posted on November 23, 2021, and the most recent update was provided on February 5, 2026.
