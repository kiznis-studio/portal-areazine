### Alert Details
The National Weather Service in Anchorage, AK, has issued a **Heavy Freezing Spray Warning** for the Kuskokwim Delta. This alert is effective starting at 5:00 PM AKST on Thursday, February 12, 2026.

### Affected Areas
The warning specifically impacts the marine zone identified as the **Kuskokwim Delta from 15 to 80 NM** (Zone PKZ764).

### What You Should Do
Mariners and residents in the affected coastal region are advised to take necessary precautions. The National Weather Service recommends that individuals **avoid** the warning area during the effective period due to the hazardous conditions.

### Expected Conditions
Weather conditions in the Kuskokwim Delta are expected to be hazardous for marine travel:
- **Freezing Spray**: Heavy freezing spray is forecast for tonight.
- **Wind**: Northwest winds at 30 knots are expected, becoming Southeast after midnight.
- **Seas**: In ice-free waters, seas are expected to reach 11 feet before subsiding to 6 feet after midnight.
- **Additional Weather**: Rain and snow are forecast for Friday, with winds remaining around 30 knots.

### Timeline
- **Warning Onset**: Thursday, February 12, 2026, at 5:00 PM AKST
- **Warning Expiration**: Friday, February 13, 2026, at 5:00 AM AKST

Conditions are expected to remain hazardous through the night, with a Small Craft Advisory also in effect through Friday night for the broader region.
